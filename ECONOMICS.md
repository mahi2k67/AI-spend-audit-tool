# ECONOMICS.md

## Product Value

AI Spend Audit Tool helps users identify unnecessary AI subscription costs and optimize monthly spending.

Users can:
- Compare AI subscription plans
- Calculate monthly overspending
- Estimate yearly savings
- Analyze team spending efficiency

## Potential Customer Segments

- Students using multiple AI tools
- Professionals paying for premium AI subscriptions
- Small businesses managing team AI expenses

## Value Proposition

Users can instantly discover whether they are overpaying for AI tools and get recommendations to reduce costs.

## Revenue Opportunities

Possible monetization in future:
- Premium advanced spending reports
- AI subscription recommendations
- Affiliate partnerships with AI tool providers
- Team audit dashboard for businesses

## Business Potential

As AI subscriptions increase, cost optimization tools can become useful for individuals and companies managing multiple AI services.
