## Interview 1
Name: Surya
Role: College Student

Problems faced:
- Uses ChatGPT and Gemini for studying and project work
- Unsure whether upgrading to paid AI plans is worth the money
- Finds it difficult to compare different AI subscription prices

Suggestions:
- Wants a simple tool to compare AI plans in one place
- Wants to know monthly and yearly savings before choosing a plan

What I learned:

- Cost transparency is important before paying for subscriptions
