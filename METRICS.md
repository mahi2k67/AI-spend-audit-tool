# Metrics

## North Star Metric

Audit Completion Rate

This measures how many users finish the full audit process and receive results.

It directly reflects product value.

## Input Metrics

### Form Start Rate

How many visitors begin entering spending data.

### Audit Completion Time

How quickly users finish the audit.

### Share Rate

How many users share their audit results.

## What I’d instrument first

- Page visits
- Form starts
- Form completions
- CTA clicks
- Time on page
- Bounce rate

## Pivot Trigger

If fewer than 20% of visitors complete the audit after 2 weeks, I would redesign onboarding and simplify the form.
